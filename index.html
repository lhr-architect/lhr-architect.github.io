About / 关于

从概念到落地，这是施工笔记。
From concept to built form, this is my construction notebook.

我是刘浩然，关注建筑、室内改造与小尺度公共空间介入，工作常常位于“设计意图”与“现场执行”之间。 I am Haoran Liu, working across architecture, interior renovation, and small-scale public space interventions.

我不仅呈现结果，也展示过程、节点逻辑与施工协作，希望让视觉秩序最终转化为空间秩序。 Beyond final imagery, I highlight process, detailing logic, and construction coordination to show how visual order becomes spatial order.
